Sentencing

Pre-Sentence

Plea OR conviction by jury sentencing

Pre-sentence investigation (PSI) 

will take place to help the judge determine the appropriate sentence. 

  • Completed by probation
  • Investigation of the defendant’s:
    • Prior criminal record
    • Family situation
    • Health 
    • Work history
    • Considers victim safety

Judge's Role

  • MN has sentencing guidelines that a judge must follow.
  • If the judge decided on a sentence outside the guidelines, it is called a departure (either upward or downward)

Victim Impact Statements

  • MN allows victims to make a statement at sentencing or to submit a written statement about the impact the crime has had on them (or in the case of murder, the impact of the crime has on the surviving family members.) 
  • These can be very powerful experiences for victims and perhaps may be the first chance the victim has had to talk openly to the court about what happened.
